About DayScript

DayScript is a personal diary web app built with Django, featuring:

  • Secure user accounts (signup/login/logout)
  • Full CRUD for diary entries
  • Moods with emoji indicators and tag organization
  • Search, filter, sort, and pagination
  • Profile stats and admin management

“Writing privately is an act of honesty. In a world that moves fast, a personal journal allows us to pause, reflect, and listen to our own voice without interruption.”
